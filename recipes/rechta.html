Rechta Recipe

Description

Rechta is a traditional Algerian dish consisting of thin and flat noodles and chicken sauce. The noodles are made with a combination of flour, salt, water, and ghee, while the sauce is made with chicken pieces, onions, garlic, oil, chickpeas, ras el hanout, cinnamon, turnips, potatoes, and zucchini.

Ingredients

for the sauce
  • Chicken thighs or drumsticks
  • A good handful of chickpeas soaked the day before
  • 1 onion
  • 250 g turnip
  • 250 g of zucchini
  • Salt pepper
  • a stick of cinnamon
  • 500 g of rechta, purchased or made home recipe
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. In a couscoussier, put the oil with the grated onion, the chicken, salt, pepper, the cinnamon.
  2. Come back a few minutes.
  3. Add water and chickpeas
  4. Cook
  5. Add 1 to 2 c to s of olive oil to fresh rechta.
  6. When the sauce starts to boil, put the rechta in the top of the couscoussier
  7. Steam it for 15 minutes
  8. Remove the rechta
  9. Put it in a large hollow dish
  10. Water it with a little cooking sauce while opening it with your fingers ( be careful it's hot )
  11. Put it back in the top of the couscoussier
  12. Cook it again for 15 minutes
  13. As soon as the chicken is cooked, remove it from the couscoussier
  14. Add the cut turnips and 5 bitter minutes joust the cut zucchini
  15. When the rechta is cooked, remove it
  16. Put it in a large hollow dish
  17. Add a little olive oil ( finally this last step is optional, I like the taste of olive oil in this dish, but you may not put )
  18. Here it is ready, serve your rela in a hollow dish
  19. Lay over the pieces of chicken, the chickpeas, the vegetables
  20. Put the cinnamon dish if you like it
